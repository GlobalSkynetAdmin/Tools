WEEK 4+: KEEP ADDING WEIGHT
The program is simple by design. Keep showing up. Keep adding weight.

Execution Rules
01
Sets Across
Use the same weight for all working sets of each lift. If you squat 100 lbs, all 3 sets are 100 lbs.
02
Linear Progression
Complete all reps with good form? Add 5 lbs to the bar next session. Deadlift: add 10 lbs.
03
Squat Depth
Below parallel — crease of the hip below top of the knee. No bouncing out of the hole.
04
Bench Strict
Feet flat on the floor. Do not bounce the bar off your chest. Full ROM, controlled descent.
05
Deadlift Pull
Slow, deliberate setup. Pull the slack out of the bar before breaking the floor. Do not heave.
06
Friday Arms Rule
No curls or skullcrushers until 3 weeks in. Then: 2 sets of 8–12 on Fridays only.
Nutrition Protocol
🥩
Eat to Grow — This Is Non-Negotiable
Rippetoe is direct: you must eat significantly more than usual to support strength gains. Focus on high-protein whole foods — ground beef, eggs, whole milk, chicken. Quality starches for energy: potatoes, rice, pasta. If you're not gaining weight, you're not eating enough. This program does not work in a caloric deficit the way fat-burn programming does.
THE FRIDAY RULE — No direct arm work (curls, skullcrushers) until you have completed at least 3 full weeks of the program. After that: 2 sets of 8–12 reps, Fridays only. The program is already working your arms through compound pressing and pulling — don't add noise before the signal is established.
