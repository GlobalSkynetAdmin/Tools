📋 Program Notes
Use the 40s Work / 20s Rest timer above for all main circuits.
Low-impact swaps are listed for any jumping movement — use them freely.
Aim for intensity over speed — controlled reps burn more than sloppy sprints.
Drink water before and after. Fat loss happens in the kitchen too — aim for a modest caloric deficit.
Progress by adding a 4th round after 3 weeks, or reducing rest intervals to 15s.
